About Us

National Degree College, Nandyal, founded by Dr. Imthiyaz Ahamed in 1994, is a beacon of education in the region. Since its inception, the college has been committed to providing quality education with a focus on innovation, discipline, and holistic development. Dr. Ahamed envisioned an institution that not only imparts knowledge but also instills values, preparing students to excel in a competitive world. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we continue to uphold this legacy.
